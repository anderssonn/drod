Credits

To explain the credits properly, I have to point out that there is an earlier version of DROD published by Webfoot Technologies, which I call "Webfoot DROD".  I received permission from Webfoot to make a free version of DROD, which I call "Caravel DROD" for the purpose of distinguishing it from Webfoot's release.  Caravel DROD was written almost entirely from scratch for its first release in 2002, and then a second "Architects' Edition" (the version of DROD you have here) was released in 2003.  I've listed people under the release(s) on which they worked, but keep in mind that contributions made in one release often carry over to the next.  This is why it is important to say what people did on versions preceding DROD:AE.  If I'm missing somebody, please e-mail bugs@drod.net, and I'll see what I can do.
