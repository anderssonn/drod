The 'Neather


The legend goes that a king of past grew tired of his annoying young son and jokingly suggested that the lad go play in the castle dungeons.  Taking his father seriously, the boy wandered deep into the dungeon chambers and was never found again.  It is said that he did not perish, but lived the rest of his life in the dungeons by preference.  Becoming a master of his new habitat, he learned all of the secrets of the underground world and formed alliances with the monsters that dwelt there.  He named himself the 'Neather and schemed to rise from the dungeons to claim the throne when his powers were able.
