Roach Queens


The Roach Queen will never attack you directly.  In fact she'll do her best to evade you so that she can concern herself with bearing deadly roach offspring.  Every thirty turns she lays eggs in all empty adjacent squares.  The eggs will hatch to full-grown dungeon roaches after just three turns, so watch out!

It is useful in some situations to know that an egg cannot be laid in a square that Beethro is standing in.
