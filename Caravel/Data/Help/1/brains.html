Brains

This ominous blob of neurons is not directly dangerous because it never moves or attacks.  The brain expands its consciousness to all of the monsters in the room so that it can communicate with them.  When an explorer enters the room, it alerts all of the monsters and gives them instructions on what paths to take to get to the intruder.

Fighting monsters endowed with the brain's intelligence becomes much harder as simple obstacles can no longer be used to prevent monsters from finding Beethro.  When all brains in a room are destroyed, the monsters will return to their normal intelligences.
